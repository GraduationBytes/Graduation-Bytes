Post-Graduation Pathways

Swipe through our curated guides on elite career routes to pursue after completing your B.Com degree.

Direct Entry Route

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

B.Com graduates with 55%+ marks bypass the Foundation exam. Register directly for CA Intermediate, complete your 4-week ICITSS training, and begin your mandatory Articleship immediately.

Corporate Law

Company Secretary (CS)

Specialise in corporate governance and board management. As a commerce graduate, you can enter the CS Executive program directly, completely bypassing the CSEET entrance test.

Tech & Finance

Corporate Readiness

The modern accounting standard. Focus heavily on mastering Tally Prime integration and advanced Microsoft Excel functions (Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP) to secure high-paying corporate roles.

Government Sector

Banking & Finance (PO)

Prepare for IBPS PO, SBI Clerk, and SSC CGL. Your B.Com background provides a massive, unfair advantage in the Quantitative Aptitude and Financial Awareness sections.
